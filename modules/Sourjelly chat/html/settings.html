Welcome to the homepage of sourjelly.net

Sourjelly is a community written by the community for the community, to explore the power of their minds, and use it in their own advantages.
people will no longer have to see the internet as a big place where data is being stored, but as their own place where they can design their personal spaces in such a manner that it represents their thoughts.

go and have a look for yourself on demo.sourjelly.net and see it working yourself.
